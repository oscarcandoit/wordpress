## Facebook Feed

EA Facebook Feed will let you display your latest Facebook posts on your WordPress site in a stunning card or overlay layout. You can style your feed in an amazing way the way you want and make it look interactive.

[Documentation](https://essential-addons.com/docs/facebook-feed/)

### Showcase Facebook Feed In Card Layout

Go to the ‘General Settings’ to choose the card layout from ‘Layout Settings’ feature and sort your content to the newest from the ‘Feed Settings’.
